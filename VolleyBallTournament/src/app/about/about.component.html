The who

Me and my group of friends. My name is Teo Jeftimov, a college student at the Zagreb University of Applied Sciences, developer and avid fan/player of beach volleyball.

The what

The biggest and most competitive beach volleyball tournament in the Podravina region of Croatia.

The why

To test ourselves against eachother, to improve ourselves, to earn money for our club, and most importantly to have fun.

The where

The tournament is held at the Tennis/volleyball club's facilities in Đurđevac, Croatia.

The when

The tournament is held every year on the 15th of August.
